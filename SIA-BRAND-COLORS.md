# SIA Brand Colors Analysis

Source: https://suddenimpactagency.io

## Primary Colors

**Magenta/Hot Pink** - Primary brand color
- Used in main headings ("AI Voice Agents")
- CTA buttons ("Try AI Voice Agent", "Schedule Demo")
- Large percentage numbers (98%, 80%, 24/7, 90%)
- Hex estimate: #FF006E or similar hot pink/magenta

**Cyan/Turquoise** - Secondary brand color
- Used in subheadings ("Business Automation Suite", "Can Measure")
- Accent text and highlights
- Hex estimate: #00D9FF or similar bright cyan

**Purple/Violet** - Tertiary brand color
- Used in gradient text ("AI Employees")
- Icon backgrounds
- Hex estimate: #8B5CF6 or similar purple

## Background

**Black** - Primary background
- Deep black background throughout the site
- Creates high contrast with bright colors

## Text Colors

**White** - Primary text color
- Body text and descriptions
- High contrast against black background

**Gray** - Secondary text color
- Supporting text and less important information

## Gradients

**Combined Gradient** - Multi-color gradient
- Magenta to Purple to Cyan
- Used for special headings and emphasis
- Creates vibrant, eye-catching effect

## Accent Colors

**Green** - Success/availability indicator
- Used for "24/7" and positive metrics
- Bright neon green (#00FF00 or similar)

## Design Notes

- High contrast design with black background
- Vibrant neon colors (magenta, cyan, purple, green)
- Modern, tech-forward aesthetic
- Heavy use of gradients for visual interest
- Icons use purple backgrounds with colored symbols
